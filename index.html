About ExpiryX

🚀 What is ExpiryX?

ExpiryX is a revolutionary token permission management system built on the Aptos blockchain. It allows you to grant time-limited spending permissions that automatically expire, providing enhanced security for your digital assets.

📋 How to Use

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Click the "Connect Wallet" button and select your Petra wallet.
  2. Grant Permission: Fill in the spender's address, token amount, and expiry duration.
  3. Select Permission Type: Choose between Full Access, Limited, or Read Only permissions.
  4. Confirm Transaction: Review and sign the transaction in your wallet.
  5. Monitor & Manage: View active permissions and revoke them if needed.

🔐 Permission Types

  • Full Access (🔓): Complete spending permissions for the specified amount
  • Limited (🔒): Restricted permissions with additional safeguards
  • Read Only (👁️): View-only access without spending capabilities

⚡ Key Features

  • 🕐 Time-Limited: Permissions automatically expire
  • 🔒 Secure: Built on Aptos blockchain technology
  • 📱 User-Friendly: Simple and intuitive interface
  • 🎯 Precise Control: Granular permission management
  • 🔄 Revocable: Cancel permissions before expiry

🛡️ Security

Your security is our priority. All transactions are executed on-chain, ensuring transparency and immutability. Private keys never leave your wallet, and all permissions are cryptographically secured.
